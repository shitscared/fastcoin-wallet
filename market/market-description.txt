Have your Fastcoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Fastcoin Wallet is the first mobile Fastcoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Fastcoin amount in FST and mFST.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Fastcoin via NFC, QR-codes or Fastcoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Fastcoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Fastcoin balance.

You can find more about Fastcoin at
http://bitcoin.org

If you want to contribute to Fastcoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/schildbach/bitcoin-wallet
